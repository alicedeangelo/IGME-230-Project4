Our concept is that of a relaxation kit. We thought of things that can be considered relaxing, and came up with three applications:
a ripple simulator, a zen garden simulator, and a weather simulator.

The ripple simulator replicates ripples in a pond. By clicking a button, the user can cause the screen to have a ripple effect.
